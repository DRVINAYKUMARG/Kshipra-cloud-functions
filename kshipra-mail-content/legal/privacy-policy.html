Important Notice

By using Kshipra, you agree to this Privacy Policy. Please go through this document before using our services.

Kshipra Study Partner Private Limited ("Kshipra", "we", "our", "us") respects your privacy.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account information: Name and email address for authentication.
  • Profile information (optional): Profile photo if you choose to upload one.
  • Usage data: Interactions with features, progress counters, and study activity within the app.
  • Device information: Device type, operating system version, app version, and unique device identifiers for performance optimization and security.
  • Mentorship data: If you book mentorship sessions, we collect session details, mentor preferences, and optional feedback.
  • Support communications: Messages you send to our support channels.

2. How We Use Information

  • To provide and maintain app functionality
  • To improve performance, reliability, and user experience
  • To facilitate mentorship bookings and communications
  • To analyze usage patterns and improve features
  • To detect and prevent fraudulent or unauthorized use
  • To communicate service-related updates and respond to support requests
  • To comply with applicable legal obligations

3. What We Don't Collect or Do

We do not:

  • Sell or rent your personal information to third parties
  • Use your data for third-party advertising or marketing
  • Collect sensitive health information or medical records
  • Track your activity outside the Kshipra app
  • Store payment card details, which are handled by payment providers
  • Collect biometric data, camera access, or device storage access
  • Use cookies, because this is a mobile app and not a website

4. Affiliates & Group Companies

We may share information with affiliates or group companies for operational purposes, subject to the same privacy protections outlined in this policy.

5. Analytics & Usage Tracking

We collect basic usage data to understand how the app is used and to improve performance. This includes tracking feature usage, session duration, and technical errors. No personally identifiable information is shared with analytics providers.

6. Anonymized & Aggregated Data

We may aggregate and anonymize usage data, so it cannot identify you, for analytics, research, and to improve our products and services.

7. Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party service providers for authentication, analytics, notifications, payment processing, and infrastructure. These providers process data in accordance with their own privacy policies and applicable laws.

8. Payment Information

Subscription Payments: Processed through Apple App Store or Google Play Store. We do not store payment card details.

Mentorship Payments: Processed through third-party payment providers such as Razorpay and Tap Payments. We receive only transaction confirmation details, not full payment credentials.

You are solely responsible for all charges incurred through payment providers. We are not liable for payment failures, errors, or security issues on third-party payment platforms.

9. External Links

Some features may open external links or third-party services, such as meeting platforms for mentorship sessions. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those services.

10. Data Storage

Data may be processed and stored on servers located outside India. We ensure that appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your information in accordance with applicable laws.

11. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect your information. However, no system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

12. Data Breach Notification

In the event of a data breach that affects your personal information, we will:

  • Notify affected users within a reasonable timeframe as required by law
  • Provide information about the nature of the breach
  • Describe steps we are taking to address the breach

However, we are not liable for security breaches beyond our reasonable control or caused by third-party service providers.

13. Data Retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary to provide the service or meet legal requirements.

14. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Withdraw consent for data processing where consent is the legal basis

To exercise these rights, contact privacy@kshiprastudypartner.com.

15. Withdrawal of Consent

You may withdraw your consent for certain data processing activities by contacting our support or Grievance Officer. Withdrawing consent may limit or disable access to features that require such data for the app to function.

16. Account Deletion

Users can request account deletion by contacting support@kshiprastudypartner.com. We process deletion requests subject to legal retention requirements and operational constraints. Upon deletion, personal data is removed from active systems in accordance with applicable law.

17. Children's Privacy

Kshipra is intended for users aged 18 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18 without parental consent. If we become aware of such collection, we will delete the information promptly.

18. Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, restructuring, or sale of assets, user information may be transferred as part of the transaction. We will provide notice if your data becomes subject to a different privacy policy.

19. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated via email or in-app notification. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

20. Grievance Redressal (India)

For grievances related to data protection or privacy, contact our Grievance Officer at grievance@kshiprastudypartner.com.

We will acknowledge grievances within seven (7) days and aim to resolve them within a reasonable timeframe, in accordance with applicable statutory requirements under the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023.

21. Governing Law

This Privacy Policy is governed by the laws of India. Any disputes shall be subject to the exclusive jurisdiction of courts in Bengaluru, Karnataka, India.
